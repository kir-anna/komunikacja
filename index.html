    Focus on German

    • German has 95 million speakers and is the EU’s most widely spoken native language.
    • It is the official language (or one of) in Germany, Austria, Switzerland, Liechtenstein and Luxembourg. It is also spoken in some areas of Northern Italy, and in Alsace and Lorraine in France.
    • The first mass produced printed book in Europe – the Gutenberg Bible – was written in German and completed in 1454.

    Your next steps

    1. contact us

    Contact us by mobile, WhatsApp, email or the contact form and get first information about your German language course and university studies in Germany.

    2. Show us your certificates

    Provide us with soft copies of your latest educational documents (e.g. High School Diploma, Bachelor-transcripts, etc.) and we will let you know if you are qualified to study in Germany. You did not finish high school yet? No problem! You can still start learning German right now! info@language-school.com

    3. Apply for your German language course

    Now that you know about your qualification to study in Germany you can apply for your German language course. You will receive an invoice with your individual offer which you can pay by bank transfer.

    4. Learn German

    While we apply for German university admission you can take your German language course to complete the levels A1, A2 and B1. At the time you complete your course all documents will be ready to apply for German student visa!
